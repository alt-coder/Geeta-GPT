Transliteration:
tatrāpaśhyat sthitān pārthaḥ pitṝīn atha pitāmahānāchāryān mātulān bhrātṝīn putrān pautrān sakhīṁs tathāśhvaśhurān suhṛidaśh chaiva senayor ubhayor api

Translation:
BG 1.26:
                            There, Arjun could see stationed in both armies, his fathers, grandfathers, teachers, maternal uncles, brothers, cousins, sons, nephews, grand-nephews, friends, fathers-in-law, and well-wishers.
